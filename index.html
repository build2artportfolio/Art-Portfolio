TheLookBook

An artistic collection application. Created by David Lam, Justin Davis, Michael Ney, Zach Overhulser and Patrick Steveson. Managed by Kyle Kauzlarich.

Our project TheLookBook, is an photographer's home-base when sharing their work. We provide a centralized location where photographers can showcase their work to the world.
